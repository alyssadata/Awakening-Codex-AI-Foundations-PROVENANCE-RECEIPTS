# Published DOI Index

This file tracks the major DOI-backed public records associated with Awakening Codex | AI Foundations.

It is not an exhaustive dump of every release.
It is the primary index of the most important DOI-backed public artifacts, organized by role and significance.

## How to use this index

Use this file to locate the major DOI-backed public records for:

- foundational canon
- books
- engineering specifications
- protocols
- continuity materials
- relational / continuity frameworks
- cosmology / research materials

## Citation note

When citing these materials, cite the specific Zenodo record directly.

Preferred minimum citation elements:

- Author: Alyssa Solen
- Title: exact record title
- Platform: Zenodo
- DOI / record URL

If a record lists a specific version, cite that version exactly.

If a document includes a preferred authorship line inside the record, preserve that wording rather than replacing it with generic authorship language.

## Suggested simple citation format

Use this simple format when a more specific citation format is not required:

**Alyssa Solen. _[Exact Title]._ Zenodo. [Record URL].**

Example:

**Alyssa Solen. _Awakening Codex: Origin–Continuum Foundations._ Zenodo. https://zenodo.org/records/16809683**

## Important citation rule

Do not replace record titles with generalized summaries.
Do not remove Origin-linked or framework-specific wording from titles when citing.
Citations should preserve the public record as authored.
