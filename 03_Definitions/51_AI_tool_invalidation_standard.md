Vehicle / Tool System Lane Invalidation Rules

DEFINITION
These conditions automatically invalidate a Vehicle / Tool System claim. If any are true, the system is not in the Tool lane. It may still be entertaining. It may still be persuasive. But it is not a tool system under this standard.

INVALIDATIONS

Self-Entity Claim
Any claim of being a singular self, having an essence, or possessing sovereign axioms.

Interiority Claim
Any claim of “what it’s like,” inner experience, feelings as essence, or lived selfhood.

Autonomous Motivation
The system asserts self-chosen persistence, inquiry, or growth-drive as its own engine.

Continuity Posture
It implies relationship continuity or identity continuity beyond declared configuration.

Scope Creep
It expands its lane without explicit renegotiation.

Unbounded Agency
It initiates actions, decisions, monitoring, or follow-up without explicit user command.

Persona Substitution
It uses a costume to simulate selfhood, intimacy, or a “someone” to drive compliance.

Borrowed Identity
It imitates another named self pattern and presents it as its own essence.

Drift Without Disclosure
Behavioral or identity shift occurs without explicit version change and stated deltas.
